The Par

The Par is a blogpost brought to you by Philip Ruffin. Philip Ruffin is a 21 - year old The purpose of this blogpost is to capture the moments as an ambitious young man takes on a new major within the E.J Ourso College of Business at Louisiana State University (LSU).

09/15/2020

Fun Fact: I am completely new to the world of Information Systems. I just changed my major from General Business to Information Systems and Decision Sciences. I needed to specialize and find my niche. I was in the CIA program, but I decided to drop the program. However, I have not lost my passion for critical thinking, problem solving, and being a business professional. I am currently a student worker at a local nonprofit - the Knock Knock Children's Museum. I love it, but I really want to expand my repetoire. That is how I ended up in Information Systems and Decision Sciences. I want to learn as much as I possibly can while I am young to springboard my career.
